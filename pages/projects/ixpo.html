Ixpo reimagines the world of photo museums with its innovative approach to augmented reality. By transforming static photographs into immersive 3D experiences, visitors can step into the realm of the photograph, engaging with it in a whole new way. With a dynamic leaderboard and a rating system, users can test their creativity and precision in recreating the photos, fostering friendly competition and a sense of achievement.
